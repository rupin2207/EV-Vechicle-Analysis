# ⚡ Electric Vehicle Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize electric vehicle (EV) trends across different states, vehicle types, manufacturers, and model years. This dashboard provides deep insights into EV adoption, average driving range, CAFV eligibility, and the market distribution of Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV).

## ✨ Key Features

* 📌 **Total Vehicle Analysis:** Displays the total volume of electric vehicles analyzed across the dataset.
* 📌 **Average EV Range:** Tracks the average electric driving range in miles to measure battery evolution.
* 📌 **BEV & PHEV Distribution:** Clear breakdown (percentage and total count) of:
* 🔹 Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV)
* 🔹 Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV)


* 📌 **Vehicles by Model Year:** Trend analysis highlighting the exponential growth of EVs over the years.
* 📌 **State-wise Vehicle Distribution:** Interactive US map visualizing spatial EV penetration and regional adoption.
* 📌 **Vehicles by Manufacturer:** Comparative breakdown of industry leaders like Tesla, Nissan, Chevrolet, Ford, BMW, and more.
* 📌 **CAFV Eligibility Analysis:** Categorization based on Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) criteria to track environmental compliance.
* 📌 **Vehicles by Model:** Tree map visualization showcasing the market's most popular EV models.
* 📌 **Dynamic Filters:** Slicers to dynamically slice and dice data by **City**, **Electric Utility**, and **Electric Vehicle Type**.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* 🔧 **Power BI Desktop** – For data modeling, DAX engineering, and dashboard design.
* 🔧 **Power Query** – For ETL processes (data cleaning, transforming, and shaping).
* 🔧 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – For creating custom measures and calculated columns.
* 🔧 **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – As the primary data source.

## 💡 Key Insights

* 📈 **Market Dominance:** Tesla heavily dominates the EV landscape with the highest volume of registered vehicles.
* 📈 **Technology Preference:** BEVs command a majority market share compared to their PHEV counterparts.
* 📈 **The Inflexion Point:** EV adoption has witnessed an unprecedented surge post-2020.
* 📈 **Regional Variation:** Specific states exhibit significantly higher EV density, highlighting infrastructure and policy readiness.
* 📈 **Compliance Tracking:** CAFV eligibility metrics effectively pinpoint top-tier environmentally compliant options.

## 🧩 Dashboard Architecture & Components

| Component | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **KPI Cards** | Real-time counter of total vehicles and overall average range |
| **Line Chart** | Historical tracking of vehicle growth mapped against model years |
| **Map Visualization** | Geospatial distribution of EVs across states |
| **Bar Chart** | Ranked comparison of manufacturers by total vehicle volume |
| **Donut Chart** | Proportionate breakdown of CAFV eligibility status |
| **Tree Map** | High-density view of individual vehicle model popularity |
